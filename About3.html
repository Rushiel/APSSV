APS SV

APS Shankar Vihar is one of the many army schools present in Delhi, it is very diverse in extra curricular activities and many students have won awards and trophies.

As the national lockdown has is taking place, children have not been able to study on a regular basis.

But our head manager, Rushiel Menon has formed a group of teachers and made an online learning platform, which will teach the APS SV students what they would have been learning before the lockdown.

"We are planning to increase the duration of the online classes" said Rushiel Menon when asked about his plans.
